Company Profile

Building the Foundation of Modern Manufacturing

Our Genesis & Growth

Founded in Ahmedabad, Gujarat—the undisputed industrial heartland of India—Aashirwad Enterprise India began with a singular vision: to eliminate the supply chain friction that prevents plastic manufacturers from scaling their operations.

What started as a localized trading post has exponentially grown into a premier, nation-wide distribution network. Today, we manage thousands of metric tons of raw polymer flow monthly, serving diverse sectors including agriculture, construction, FMCG packaging, and automotive components.

Our growth is not measured merely in tonnage, but in the trust we've cemented with over 250 active manufacturing partners who rely on our materials every single day.

The Aashirwad Advantage

Zero-Defect Quality Policy

Every batch of resin and chemical additive is subject to stringent verification. We only supply prime, virgin materials unless specifically requested for industrial-grade applications.

Financial Resilience

Our robust financial architecture allows us to maintain massive inventories, shielding our partners from sudden price spikes in the global crude and petrochemical markets.

Client-Centric Logistics

We adapt to your factory's intake capabilities, offering customized packaging from standard 25kg bags to massive bulk tanker deliveries.

Our Core Principles

Integrity

Transparent pricing, honest lead times, and absolute clarity regarding material origins and specifications. We do not compromise on truth.

Reliability

When we commit to a delivery schedule, it happens. Our logistical redundancies ensure that your production line never halts waiting for our trucks.

Innovation

Constantly upgrading our portfolio to include new, highly efficient chemical additives and specialized polymer grades that improve end-product performance.

Powering the plastic industry with unmatched reliability, quality, and technical expertise.
