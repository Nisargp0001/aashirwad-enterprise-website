Corporate Structure

Diversified Excellence in Polymer Supply

Aashirwad Enterprise India operates through three specialized, highly integrated business divisions. Together, they create a flawless pipeline from global petrochemical refineries directly to the factory floors of our manufacturing partners.

01. Bulk Polymer Trading Wing

Our flagship division handles the massive-scale procurement and distribution of commodity thermoplastics. We bridge the gap between volatile international petrochemical markets and domestic manufacturers who require price stability and guaranteed supply.

Strategic Procurement

Leveraging high-volume purchasing power to secure preferred allocations from domestic and international giants (Reliance, Opal, ONGC, and global suppliers).

Market Hedging

Utilizing deep market intelligence to advise clients on procurement timing, insulating them from sudden crude oil price shocks or supply chain disruptions.

Just-In-Time (JIT) Delivery

Our warehouse infrastructure is optimized for JIT frameworks, allowing manufacturers to drastically reduce their own inventory carrying costs.

Grade Selection Consulting

Matching specific Melt Flow Indices (MFI) and tensile properties to the client's exact machinery and end-product requirements.

02. Specialty Chemicals & Additives

Modern plastics require advanced chemistry to meet regulatory, environmental, and performance standards. This highly technical division supplies the critical additives that transform raw resins into specialized, high-value materials.

  • A

    Plasticizers & Stabilizers

    Essential for PVC compounding to ensure flexibility (for cables/hoses) and prevent thermal degradation during extrusion.

  • B

    UV Protectors & Antioxidants

    Critical formulations for outdoor applications (agricultural films, water tanks) to prevent polymer chain breakdown from sunlight exposure.

  • C

    Color Masterbatches

    High-dispersion pigment concentrates for uniform coloration without compromising the mechanical integrity of the base resin.

03. Logistics & Supply Chain Infrastructure

Operating from the industrial heartland of Gujarat, our logistics wing ensures that our promises of "On-Time Delivery" are mathematically guaranteed. We own the movement of our materials from port to plant.

Mass Storage Facilities: Our primary warehouses in Ahmedabad cover tens of thousands of square feet, equipped with advanced material handling equipment to safely store sensitive polymers in moisture-controlled environments.

Integrated Fleet Operations: We bypass third-party delays by utilizing dedicated freight channels. Whether it is 25kg standardized bags, jumbo bags, or bulk loose material shipments, we have the fleet capacity to move it safely and legally.

Real-Time Tracking: Utilizing modern ERP systems to give clients absolute transparency on their dispatch schedules and ETA.

Powering the plastic industry with unmatched reliability, quality, and technical expertise.
