Premium Resins & Specialty Chemicals

Our extensive portfolio is curated to support the most demanding manufacturing applications. From high-tensile piping to ultra-clear food packaging, we supply the precise chemical formulations required for absolute structural perfection.

Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC)

A highly versatile, cost-effective thermoplastic polymer widely used in construction, healthcare, and IT infrastructure. We supply Suspension and Emulsion PVC resins tailored to your specific K-value requirements.

Available Grades & Applications:

  • K-67 / K-65: Optimal for rigid pipes, conduits, and heavy-duty profiles.
  • K-57 / K-58: High flow resins designed for complex injection-molded fittings.
  • Emulsion Paste Grades: Used for artificial leather, flooring, and rotational molding.

High & Low-Density Polyethylene

The backbone of the global packaging industry. We provide polyethylene grades that balance environmental stress-cracking resistance (ESCR) with excellent processability and clarity.

Available Grades & Applications:

  • HDPE Blow Molding: Ideal for rigid containers, jerry cans, and drums.
  • HDPE Injection Molding: High impact strength for crates, pallets, and household wares.
  • LDPE Film Grades: Superior clarity and stretch for shrink wraps, heavy-duty sacks, and agricultural films.
Request Tech Data Sheet

Polypropylene (PP)

Known for its tough, heat-resistant, and semi-rigid properties, Polypropylene is critical for everything from automotive interiors to woven sacks. We stock homopolymer, random copolymer, and block copolymer grades.

Available Grades & Applications:

  • Raffia Grade: Exceptional tensile strength for woven fabrics, cement bags, and ropes.
  • Injection Grade: Fast cycle times for thin-wall packaging and consumer appliances.
  • Copolymer Grades: Enhanced impact resistance for battery cases and automotive bumpers.
